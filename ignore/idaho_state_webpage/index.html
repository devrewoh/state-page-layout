Idaho

Idaho is in the northwestern region within the United States. Its known for its outdoor recreational activities such as white-water rafting and skiing, its scenic landscapes and its famous high-quality potatoes.

Idaho is nested between multiple states such as Washington, Oregon, Nevada, Utah, Wyoming and Montana. It even borders Canada on it' northern panhandle! Idaho has a history of adventure and exploration as it was uncovered by early pioneers during the Lewis and Clark Expedition.

The current running population estimate of Idaho is 1,964,726 as of July 1, 2022.
